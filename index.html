Blue Copper Chess Club

Blue Copper Chess club is a small, casual club that meets at Blue Copper 2000 in Salt Lake City. We play from 8 A.M. to 10 A.M. every Thursday morning over donuts and coffee. Players of any age and skill level are welcome, and we will always keep a casual, non-competitive environment.


Beyond Thursday mornings, stay tuned for events and tournaments that will happen often! We hope to see you over the board.

A Little About Us

B.C.C.C. was founded somewhere between February and April 2022 by a former Blue Copper Coffee employee and a handful of other people interested in playing chess. We're on Instagram, which is the best place to get in touch with us if you have any questions!
